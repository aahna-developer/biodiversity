Biodiversity

Earth provides enough to satisfy every man's needs, but not every man's greed- MAHATMA GANDHI

The diverse variety of plants, animals along with microorganism living in a particular region is called biodiversity.

Importance of Biodiversity

Biodiversity includes a huge variety of plants and animals that live in a natural habitat and are mutually dependent. Biodiversity maintains the environment around us. We are also economically dependent upon biodiverstiy. We get variety of food items, cloth items, wood, medicines, oils from variety of plants. We get food, leather, milk from animals.

Causes of Loss of Biodiversity

  1. Deforestation
  2. Illegal hunting
  3. Pollution
  4. Natural disasters
  5. Exotic species
