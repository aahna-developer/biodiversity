Conservation of Biodiversity

We should conserve forests and wildlife to preserve biodiversity to prevent endangered species from becoming extinct and to maintain ecological balance in nature.

Some of the measures which can conserve forest and wildlife are given below.

  • Deforestation
  • Illegal hunting
  • Pollution

Protected Areas

Protected Areas are undisturbed habitats of wildlife. For conserving and maintaining biological diversity, areas both on land and in water are protected through legal laws and other means. National parks, wildlife sanctuaries and biosphere reserves are some of the protected areas created by our Government.
